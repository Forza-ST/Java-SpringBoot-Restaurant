Our Story

Established in 2020, Forza Restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Italy to Washington, DC. Our chef, trained in Florence, creates dishes using traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients.

We take pride in offering a warm, welcoming atmosphere where you can enjoy a memorable dining experience.

Our Cuisine

At Forza, we specialize in traditional Italian dishes with a modern twist. From our handmade pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, every dish is prepared with passion and care.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible and import specialty items directly from Italy.
